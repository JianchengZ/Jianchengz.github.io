Biography

I am a first-year PhD student in the ECE department at UC Riverside, supervised by Prof. Yinglun Zhu. Before this, I earned a BS in Software Engineering at Shandong University. During my undergraduate studies, I did some research on the application of machine learning, being advised by Prof. Yonghui Xu, and I also explored some biology problems using machine learning, being advised by the Assistant Professor at New York University: Dr. Mingtao Xia.

My research area is machine learning, particularly in developing active learning strategies to enhance data and computational efficiency while maintaining model performance. Additionally, I am also interested in other machine learning methods and their applications in healthcare, biology, and other fields.
